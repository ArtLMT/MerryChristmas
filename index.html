Merry Christmas

Do Thao Uyen

You’ve weathered a year of trials, my love, And though it may not have unfolded as you dreamed, You gave it your all, pouring your heart into every moment. Your hard work is a seed, destined to blossom with time, And I admire you deeply for that. As the holiday lights glow softly around us, I wish you warmth, joy, and the comfort of love, To embrace you as you rest and renew your spirit. Stay strong, my dearest, and keep chasing your goal For I know you’ll reach them, one step at a time.
